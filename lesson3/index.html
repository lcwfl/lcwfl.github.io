Site Plan

This is the Site Plan for the FL Weather Tracker website.

Domain Name

I found the domain name of flweathertracker.com on GoDaddy for $11 a year.

Site purpose

The purpose of this website is to give users around the world a place to track weather systems, such as hurricanes, and have open discussions and predictions as to what the affects of the systems will have. Since I live in Florida and it's getting close to hurricane season, I chose the name of flweathertracker.com. Users will be able to see the current predictions and engage in discussions with other enthusiasts.
