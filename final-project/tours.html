Things to do in Cozumel

There are many different tour opportunities available in the Cozumel area, easily reachable on one of our rentals. From laying on the beach at Paradise Beach, to snorkeling with stingrays, Cozumel has it all.

Paradise Beach

Located on the western side of the island, both locals and tourists alike spend their days soaking up the sun and enjoying the soft sand. As such, be prepared for large crowds. Getting there early can help stake out your spot where you can relax and swim in the beautifaul waters.

Stingray Beach

Stingray Beach is located in San Miguel. Here you can get up close and personal with stingrays and other underwater aquatic life. Don't worry about rays though, they have had their barbs removed so they won't harm you. Like Paradise Beach, be prepared for crowds and plan on getting their early. Don't worry though, our stores open early enough to ensure you get there ontime.
